How It's Calculated

The optimizer balances two constraints to find the ideal number of trades:

1. Commission Cap: Ensures total commissions don't exceed 5% of your capital.

2. Trade Viability: Ensures each trade is large enough to buy at least one share (for whole shares) or is greater than the commission fee (for fractional shares).

The price-drop trigger is then calculated based on the stock's volatility and the optimal number of trades:

$$ \text{Trigger Percentage} = \frac{\text{Annualized Volatility}}{\sqrt{\text{Optimal Trades}}} $$
