How It's Calculated

The calculator first determines your capital gain and the tax you'd owe:

$$\text{Capital Gain} = \text{Current Value} - \text{Cost Basis}$$ $$\text{Tax Cost} = \text{Capital Gain} \times \text{Tax Rate}$$

Then, it calculates the required return on a new investment to cover the tax cost:

$$\text{Required Return} = \frac{\text{Tax Cost}}{\text{Current Value} - \text{Tax Cost}}$$
